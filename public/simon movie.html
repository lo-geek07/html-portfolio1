MY TOP 3 ANIME OF ALL TIME

ATTACK ON TITAN (AOT)

This is my favourite anime so far, I love the plot, storyline, animation quality and overall character development.
My favourite character in the anime is LEVI ACKERMAN.

DEMON SLAYER

This was one of the first anime I watched, and it made me show interest in anime generally.
The storyline and animation quality were one of the things that made me to love the anime so much.
My favourite character is KYOJURO RENGOKU.

JUJUTSU KAISEN (JJK)

To round up my top 3 is this anime, the animators must have really worked hard on the choreography of the fight scenes.
My favourite character is SATORU GOJO.
